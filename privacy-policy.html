Privacy Policy

Last updated: [27/7/2026]

This Privacy Policy explains how AgeCoin.org ("we," "our," "the site") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit this website.

1. Information We Collect

We aim to collect as little personal information as possible. Depending on how you use the site, we may collect:

  • Technical data such as browser type, device type, and pages visited, gathered automatically through standard web server logs and/or analytics tools.
  • Information you provide directly, such as an email address if you sign up for updates, or a message if you contact us.
  • Public blockchain data, such as wallet addresses, if you interact with the AgeCoin protocol. Blockchain transactions are public and outside our control once submitted.

2. How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used to operate and improve the site, respond to inquiries, and communicate updates about AgeCoin to people who opt in. We do not sell personal information.

3. Third-Party Links

This site may link to third-party services (exchanges, block explorers, social platforms, wallet providers). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties — review their own policies before using them.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information we hold, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for the security of your own wallet and private keys — we will never ask for your private keys or seed phrase.

5. Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you by contacting us at [agecoin.xyz@gmail.com].

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date above.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to [agecoin.xyz@gmail.com].
