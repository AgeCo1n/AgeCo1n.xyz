Frequently Asked Questions

What is AgeCoin?

AgeCoin (AGE) is a cryptocurrency secured by Proof-of-Age consensus, a validation model that rewards validators for sustained, honest participation over time instead of raw computing power.

How does Proof-of-Age consensus work?

Validators build trust the longer they holding honestly on the network. That accumulated trust — not hardware muscle — determines validation weight and rewards, which keeps energy use low.
